About

SPERIXLABS is a Cybersecurity Research Center. The research center conducts research in the technologies and techniques which will secure information systems of tomorrow. We also work at the intersectino of application development and offensive engineering to help companies build secure code.

The Lab conducts the following security assessments:

  1. Web Applications and APIs
  2. Mobile Applications
  3. Desktop & Server Applications
  4. Cloud Security
  5. Reverse Engineering
  6. Smart Contracts

We uncover vulnerabilities that may elude others and contribute to risk reduction by offering practical solutions. In essence, we are an offensive security firm that operates with the perspective of a blue team.

Backed by years of experience in both major tech corporations and startups, we are adept at conducting thorough examinations of extensive codebases. Our methods include manual source code auditing and dynamic testing, carried out by security engineers who combine expertise with precision.

Our mission is to instill trust in our clients’ products and enhance the robustness of the digital landscape.
