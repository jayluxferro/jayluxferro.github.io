About

SPERIXLABS is a Cybersecurity Research Lab. The research lab conducts research in the technologies and techniques which will secure information systems of tomorrow.

The Lab conducts research in the following areas:

  1. Internet of Things (IoT).
  2. Network and Application Security.
  3. Applied Artificial Intelligence.
  4. Applied Crytography.

The lab also performs the following activities:

  • Application Security Testing (Web, Mobile and Desktop).
  • Network Penetration Testing.
